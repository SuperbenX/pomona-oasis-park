POP · A Vibrant Lifestyle Oasis for Harare

POP is not just “a place to buy things.” It is a lifestyle destination that shapes how people spend their day, week, and weekend. It cares about who people come with, how long they stay, and what kind of memories they create.

As an urban oasis, POP offers a cleaner, safer and more comfortable environment for families, young people and professionals. As a pop culture stage, it hosts markets, music and cultural content worth sharing. As a commercial engine, it creates real business opportunities for retailers, POP Build tenants, brands, and investors.

Our vision is to become Harare’s most vibrant and influential lifestyle destination – an urban living room where commerce, culture and ecology come together for a more prosperous, sustainable and human-centered city.

Modern, Clean & Safe

Business Thrives, Culture Lives, Lifestyle Happens

POP is designed as a three-in-one platform: a business ecosystem, a cultural generator, and a lifestyle destination. It supports revenue, content and experience in a single, coherent environment.

For retailers and brands, POP offers a gateway into the Zimbabwean market with professional leasing, transparent contracts and long-term operational support. For POP Build tenants, it upgrades traditional building materials markets into a branded, clustered and experience-driven industrial environment.

  • Mixed-use retail: daily needs, lifestyle, F&B and services.
  • POP Build: a modern, professional building materials cluster.
  • Anchor-ready large-format box for supermarket / wholesale.

  • Content and cultural programming across the year.

Location & Catchment

Located in Pomona in northern Harare, POP sits at the intersection of growing residential communities, major arterial roads and a naturally strong building materials catchment.

For visitors, POP becomes a convenient, safe and enjoyable “where to go this weekend” answer. For merchants and brands, it offers stable, quality traffic, extended dwell time and a strong long-term growth story.

  • Strategic northern Harare node with city-wide reach.
  • Easy access from key residential neighborhoods and workplaces.
  • Natural link between everyday retail and POP Build trade traffic.
  • Future-ready as Harare continues to expand northwards.

Anchor Tenant Opportunity
