Project Overview

POP · Pomona Oasis Park is a retail-led mixed-use development designed as a long-term commercial platform for northern Harare, integrating lifestyle retail, POP Build (building materials), anchor retail and public spaces into one coherent destination.

A New Commercial Destination for Harare

POP is not positioned as a traditional enclosed shopping mall, nor as a conventional building materials market. Instead, it is conceived as an open, low-rise, accessible commercial destination that reflects the way people live, shop and work in Harare today.

By combining everyday retail, food and beverage, POP Build and large-format anchor opportunities, POP creates multiple layers of footfall, longer dwell time and stronger commercial resilience across economic cycles.

Vision & Long-term Strategy

POP is developed with a long-term operational mindset rather than a short-term leasing approach. The project prioritizes sustainable tenant mix, operational efficiency and brand credibility, creating a stable environment for merchants, partners and investors.

As an urban oasis, POP provides a cleaner, safer and more organized commercial environment. As a lifestyle destination, it supports events, markets and community-driven activities. As a commercial platform, it enables businesses to grow with structure, visibility and professional management.

Development Timeline

POP is planned and executed in phases to ensure quality delivery, controlled leasing and smooth operational ramp-up.

  • Concept development & master planning completed
  • Leasing and anchor engagement in progress
  • Construction and fit-out phase to follow
  • Target opening: Late 2026 – Q1 2027

Why Pomona · Why Now

Located in Pomona, northern Harare, POP benefits from strong residential growth, improving infrastructure and a naturally established building materials catchment.

As Harare continues to expand northwards, POP is positioned to serve both everyday convenience needs and destination-driven visits, creating long-term relevance and commercial value.
