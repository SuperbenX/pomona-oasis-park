Leasing Zones Overview

POP · Pomona Oasis Park is organized into clearly defined leasing zones. Each zone is designed to support specific business formats, customer flows and long-term operational performance.

Zone 1 · Dining & Social Hub

The Dining & Social Hub is designed as the lifestyle and social heart of POP. It brings together dining, leisure and social experiences in a highly visible, destination-driven environment that encourages longer dwell time and repeat visits.

This zone is curated for food and beverage brands that value atmosphere, social interaction and brand expression, supporting both daytime activity and a vibrant evening economy.

  • Restaurants, casual dining and signature food concepts
  • Cafés, lounges and social-driven formats
  • Bars and evening-oriented lifestyle venues

Ideal for brands seeking destination positioning, strong social visibility and a setting that supports both dining and nightlife experiences.

Zone 2 · POP Build
Building Materials Cluster

POP Build is a purpose-designed building materials and home improvement cluster, bringing together related trades in a structured, safe and professional commercial environment.

The cluster is planned to upgrade traditional fragmented markets into a more organized destination, improving customer experience, operational efficiency and brand credibility for merchants.

  • Tiles, timber, sanitary ware and fittings
  • Hardware, tools and construction-related trades
  • Home improvement and project-oriented suppliers

Ideal for brands seeking destination positioning, strong social visibility and a setting that supports both dining and nightlife experiences.

Zone 3 · Anchor Platform
Large-format Opportunity

The Anchor Platform at POP is planned as a large-format commercial opportunity designed to support a key anchor tenant that can serve as a consistent daily traffic driver for the wider development.

The platform is suitable for supermarket, wholesale or other large-scale operators seeking long-term presence, strong accessibility and regional reach in northern Harare.

  • Large-format retail or wholesale concepts
  • Flexible configuration to suit operational requirements
  • Direct access, parking convenience and high visibility

Ideal for brands seeking destination positioning, strong social visibility and a setting that supports both dining and nightlife experiences.

Detailed unit layouts, technical drawings and availability information are shared upon enquiry to ensure alignment with tenant requirements.

How Availability Is Managed

To protect tenant value and long-term performance, POP does not release all units to the market at once. Leasing availability reflects current phase planning, tenant mix strategy and active negotiations.

Specific unit sizes, locations and commercial terms are discussed directly with qualified tenants to ensure alignment with the overall positioning of the project.
